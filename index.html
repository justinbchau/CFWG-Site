Get Started

Welcome to CrossFit Willow Glen!

CrossFit Willow Glen offers everyone an opportunity to workout in an atmosphere similar to that found on a sports team. We're a gym where you know everyone and where everyone you train with will welcome you, encourage you, and push you to be your best. We have an atmosphere that encourages everyone to "battle" the workout together and come out the other side a better person: physically, mentally, and even spiritually.

Let us help you get in the best shape of your life! All classes are given under the watchful eyes of experienced coaches and in classes that won't make you feel like you are a sardine swimming with a school of a thousand other sardines!

Why you do CrossFit and how do you use CF outside of the gym I do CrossFit because my passion is fitness and health and CrossFit gives me all of that and also the addition of community. I have always been an athlete and before Crossfit I was always in the gym lifting weights and running outside (and did those by myself). When I found CrossFit, I found out that I was able to get those 2 things and so much more in a fitness environment of varying intensity, variety, friendly competition and that sense of being part of a something. I use CrossFit outside of the gym by having it as my main training tool for staying fit and for my Masters Track and Field competitions. CrossFit allows me to strengthen all aspects of my body and fitness, which helps me for my competition running, and for my general health in everyday life. Why you LOVE CFWG and how you found out about it yourself I found CFWG in 2012 when a friend of mine recommended I try it. I have to say that I was skeptical and was happy (I thought) with my current workout regimen. When I finally got talked into it, I was introduced to Audra and CFWG with an introductory class and I was hooked. I signed up, took the On Ramp class and I was off and running at CFWG, being one of the many in the "early crew" at 5am. It was such an inviting environment and everyone was so friendly and welcoming, and I felt like I had been part of the family for a while. Now, so many years later, it still feels that same way. Why you chose to coach CrossFit I chose to coach Crossfit for a few reasons, one of the main one being that I wanted to give back to the community the passion that I felt each time I stepped into the CrossFit gym and did the workout of the day. The other was to show that anyone can do this, with proper training, especially anyone who is 50+ (as I am). I found out quickly that as an older athlete (or any athlete for that matter) there are "rules" your body needs to follow, whether you like it or not. Sometimes you have setbacks such as injuries, which I have had my share, and the ability to come back from those is as important as following a program when you are healthy. To be able to impart some of that "wisdom" is also a reason why I coach. Misc. things you want to add. Being a CFWG coach and athlete helps me understand how Crossfit fits into my fitness plan and also has allowed me to better understand how it could fit into someone's own plan for their fitness. I currently coach my co-workers at the company where I work (a start up in the hi tech industry) with 2 classes per week .This allows me to coach individuals that may not think they are athletes (they are) and to help them realize a level of fitness they may not have thought about or thought was possible. .
