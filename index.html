Welcome to CrossFit Willow Glen!

CrossFit Willow Glen offers everyone an opportunity to workout in an atmosphere similar to that found on a sports team. We're a gym where you know everyone and where everyone you train with will welcome you, encourage you, and push you to be your best. We have an atmosphere that encourages everyone to "battle" the workout together and come out the other side a better person: physically, mentally, and even spiritually.

Let us help you get in the best shape of your life! All classes are given under the watchful eyes of experienced coaches and in classes that won't make you feel like you are a sardine swimming with a school of a thousand other sardines!
